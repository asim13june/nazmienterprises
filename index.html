Our Story & Commitment

**Nazmi Enterprises** has been a pillar of interior design and construction in **Mumbai** since its founding. We specialize in precision **Gypsum Work**, from intricate **POP Molding** to robust **Drywall Partitions**. Our commitment is simple: use high-grade materials and exceptional craftsmanship to create stunning, long-lasting spaces that meet our clients' exact needs.

We are adept at handling both large commercial projects, where **Acoustic Panels** are crucial for sound management, and detailed residential renovations requiring sophisticated **False Ceilings** and **Repair & Restoration** services. We don't just build; we craft environments.
